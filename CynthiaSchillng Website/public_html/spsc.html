SPSC logo & identity

I was invited to design the logo & identity for Saima Park String Circle, a new local music ensemble that was formed during the pandemic, when orchestras had to shut down. The ensemble plays outdoors, and musicians stand in a large circle, spaced ten feet apart. Here's some of my process work and sketches:


Here are some of my initial logo concepts. Some represent the strings on the instruments, others the circle the musicians stand in, and others the friendly, welcoming nature of the ensemble. I presented the logos to the ensemble director and musicians, and they liked on the one with dots forming the circle.


Next, I refined the logo, and fine-tuned spacing, size, color, and font choice. For the font, I looked for one that was friendly and welcoming but still professional—and ultimately chose Europa typeface.
