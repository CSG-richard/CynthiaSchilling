Hiking Mt. Wachusett

To create this visual story, I photographed a hike, capturing it from different perspectives and viewpoints to represent the many different experiences people can have hiking. The overall design moves from the bottom left to top right, in a stair-like way, to resemble the uphill hike. 11 x 36 in.
