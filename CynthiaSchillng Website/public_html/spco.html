Saima Park Community Orchestras
Welcome to the newly-formed Saima Park Community Orchestras, which take place in 4000 square foot outdoor covered pavilion that has lighting and sound system. We will start with string orchestras that are socially-distanced (with masks, and one person per stand with separation). Open to all ages and levels. Currently have two divisions: Saima String Circle, and Saima Youth Ensemble.
