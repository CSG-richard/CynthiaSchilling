FSU Orchestra designs

Here are some designs I created for the Fitchburg State University Orchestra and FSU Bands. The poster was for a combined orchestra and band concert. In the poster, the individual instrument pieces coming together to create a cohesive composition represents the way the individual musicians join together to form a cohesive music ensemble.

In this motion graphic work that advertises an orchestra concert, fragments assemble together to form a cohesive design. The pieces symbolize orchestra musicians coming together to create cohesive sounds.
