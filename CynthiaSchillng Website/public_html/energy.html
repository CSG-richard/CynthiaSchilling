Energy

I created a motion graphic video that represents the concept of energy, using video and animated text. I chose to use a video of my performance of Shostakovich's Prelude & Fugue no. 15 in D-Flat Major. The piano piece is complex and required months of intense physical and mental energy to learn, memorize, and perform. The atonality and metrical instability creates chaos, while the conjunction of the fast tempo and constant quarter notes creates unrelenting energy. The layers of overlapping text and images, in synch with the music, depict the perplexity of the fugue.
