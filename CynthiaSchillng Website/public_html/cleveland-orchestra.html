Cleveland Orchestra Identity

For a class, we were assigned to create a new logo and brand identity for an organization of our choosing. I chose to create a logo and brand identity that aligns with the Cleveland Orchestra's mission to convey a more modern, playful, and dynamic approach to the classical concert setting.
